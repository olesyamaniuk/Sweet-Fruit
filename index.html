How to Play

  • Match Fruits:

    Drag and slide three or more identical fruits to create a match and clear them from the board.

  • Complete Targets:

    Each level has unique goals—focus on achieving them within the given moves.

  • Use Boosters:

    Tap into a variety of boosters, including bombs and hammers, to tackle difficult levels.

  • Collect Rewards:

    Spin the daily wheel and participate in events for exciting prizes.

  • Compete with Friends:

    Connect your account to compare scores and compete for the top spot.

Tips for Success:

  • Start with fruits at the edges to open up more matching possibilities.
  • Save power-ups for the toughest levels.
  • Keep an eye on your move count to maximize efficiency.

FAQ

  • Is Sweet Fruit Match Puzzle Blast free to play?

    Yes, it's 100% free to download and play, with optional in-app purchases for boosters and extras.

  • Do I need an internet connection?

    No, the game supports offline play, so you can enjoy it anytime, anywhere.

  • What are the rewards for spinning the wheel?

    Rewards include coins, boosters, and other in-game items to help you progress.

  • How do I unlock new levels?

    Complete the current level's objectives to unlock the next one.

  • Are there daily challenges or events?

    Yes, participate in limited-time events and daily challenges to earn special rewards.

    You can use coins to purchase extra moves or restart the level and try again.

    Absolutely! Connect the game to your social accounts to see how you rank among friends.

    Bombs and hammers are great for clearing large sections of the board quickly. Use them strategically to overcome tough levels.
